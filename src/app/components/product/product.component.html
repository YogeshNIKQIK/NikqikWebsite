Why Choose Our Integration?

Our integration tool allows you to seamlessly connect Salesforce with Freshdesk, automating the creation and updating of cases directly within Salesforce. No more switching between systems or manual data entry. With just one click, you can synchronize information between both platforms, ensuring accuracy and saving valuable time.

KEY FEATURES

One-Click Case Creation

Easily create a case in Salesforce directly from Freshdesk tickets with a single click.

Automatic Field Mapping

Our integration automatically maps fields between Freshdesk and Salesforce, eliminating the need for manual input and reducing errors.

Real-Time Updates

Changes made in Freshdesk are instantly reflected in Salesforce, keeping both systems in perfect sync.

Getting Started is Simple

Installing our integration app is straightforward. Once installed, a new button will appear within your Freshdesk interface. Clicking this button creates a case in Salesforce using the mapped fields from Freshdesk tickets. It’s that easy!
